Welcome To WAR!

Rules

1 credit to play

One card is given to the player and dealer. The individual with the higher numbered card wins. If the player card and dealer card are of the same number, WAR occurs. During WAR the player and dealer are given new cards to compare.
  • Win earns 2 credits
  • WAR win earns 3 credits
  • WAR lost lose 1 credit
